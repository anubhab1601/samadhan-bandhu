# NEW APPLICATION FORM - FIELD REQUIREMENTS

## Based on 6 Images Provided

### **FORMAT I: VILLAGE LEVEL DATA**

#### 1. Village Level Data
- State (Name + LGD Code)
- District (Name + LGD Code)
- Block (Name + LGD Code)
- Gram Panchayat (Name + LGD Code)
- Village (Name + LGD Code)
- Population of Village (according to census, 2011)
- SC Population of Village (according to census, 2011)
- No. of households in Village (according to census, 2011)
- No. of households Surveyed in the Need Assessment/Household Survey
- Period of Need Assessment/Household Survey (From - To dates)

#### 2. GPS Data
- GPS Coordinates of Village
  - Latitude
  - Longitude

#### 3. Details of Village PMAGY Convergence Committee
- Table with columns:
  - Name
  - Designation
  - Mobile Number
  - Email
  - Address
  - Remarks
- Multiple rows for committee members

### **FORMAT II: INFRASTRUCTURE DEVELOPMENT & ACTION PLAN**

#### **DOMAIN 1: DRINKING WATER AND SANITATION**

**1.1 Monitorable Indicator:** Whether there are adequate sustainable drinking water sources to cover the village available?
- Status: Yes/No
- Details of action plan (2 rows)

**1.2 Monitorable Indicator:** % of households provided with clean drinking water
- No. of households in the Village
- No. of households provided with clean drinking water
- No. of households not provided with clean drinking water
- Details of action plan (2 rows)

**1.4 Monitorable Indicator:** Have toilets been provided in all Schools & Anganwadis in the village?
- Number of Schools in Village
- Number of Anganwadis in Village
- Number of Schools having toilets
- Number of Anganwadis having toilets
- Number of Schools where toilets are to be constructed
- Number of Anganwadis where toilets are to be constructed
- Names of Schools where toilets are to be constructed
- Names of Anganwadis where toilets are to be constructed
- Details of action plan (2 rows)

**1.6 Monitorable Indicator:** % of drains available along all internal roads
- Total length (in mts.) of internal roads in the village
- Total length (in mts.) of drains already available along internal roads
- Total length (in mts.) of drains still to be constructed along internal roads
- Details of action plan (2 rows)

**1.7 Monitorable Indicator:** % of existing drains functioning
- Total length (in mts.) of drains already available along internal roads
- Total length (in mts.) of drains functional
- Total length (in mts.) of drains to be made functional
- Details of action plan (2 rows)

**1.8 Monitorable Indicator:** % of solid and liquid waste being disposed effectively
- Total volume of Solid waste (in litres) generated during a month
- Total volume of Liquid waste (in litres) generated during a month
- Volume of Solid waste (in litres) disposed properly in a month
- Volume of Liquid waste (in litres) disposed properly in a month
- Volume of Solid waste (in litres) disposal capacity still to be added
- Volume of Liquid waste (in litres) disposal capacity still to be added
- Details of action plan (2 rows)
